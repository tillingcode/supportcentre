Key Benefits You May Be Entitled To

Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Help with extra costs of long-term ill health or disability. Not means-tested. Can be claimed whether working or not.

Employment & Support Allowance (ESA)

Financial support if you're unable to work due to illness or disability. Includes work capability assessment.

Universal Credit

Single payment covering living costs, housing and more. Extra amounts available for health conditions and caring responsibilities.

Carer's Allowance

For those caring for someone at least 35 hours a week. Currently ¬£81.90/week (2024/25).

Attendance Allowance

For people of State Pension age who need help with personal care due to illness or disability.

Breathing Space (Debt Respite)

60-day protection from creditors if you're getting mental health crisis treatment. Freezes interest and charges.
